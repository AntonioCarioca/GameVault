The Super Nintendo Entertainment System, commonly shortened to Super Nintendo, Super NES or SNES, is a 16-bit home video game console developed by Nintendo in Japan, it is called the Super Famicom (SFC). The Super NES is Nintendo's second programmable home console, following the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) console introduced advanced graphics and sound capabilities.
