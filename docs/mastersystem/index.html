The Master System is an 8-bit third-generation home video game console manufactured and developed by Sega. It was originally a remodeled export version of the Sega Mark III, the third iteration of the SG-1000 series of consoles. The original Master System models use both cartridges and a credit card-sized format known as Sega Cards.
