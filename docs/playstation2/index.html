The PlayStation 2 (PS2) is a home video game console developed and marketed by Sony Computer Entertainment. It is the successor to the PlayStation, as well as the second installment in the PlayStation brand of consoles. The PlayStation 2 received widespread critical acclaim upon release. A total of over 4,000 game titles were released worldwide, with over 1.5 billion copies sold.
